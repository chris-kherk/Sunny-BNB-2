Dog Friendly Properties

Sunny BNB

We do offer guests the opportunity to bring their dog with them at some of our. properties. Offering dog-friendly short-stay holiday properties can be a great decision for both guests and property owners for some particular properties.

In recent years, there has been a growing trend in pet ownership, and many people consider their pets as members of their family. Therefore, making your holiday property dog-friendly can help to attract more guests, increase bookings, and differentiate your property from others in the market.

Read Post

One of the main advantages for guests is that they can bring their beloved pets along with them on holiday, rather than leaving them at home or in kennels. This can be particularly beneficial for those who have anxiety or stress related to leaving their pets behind. Additionally, guests can avoid the additional expense of hiring a pet sitter or kennel. Having a dog-friendly property can also encourage guests to spend more time outdoors, exploring the local area with their furry companions. This can lead to a more enjoyable and memorable holiday experience for guests, which can translate into positive reviews and repeat bookings.

For property owners, offering a dog-friendly short-stay holiday property can be advantageous in several ways. Firstly, it can increase bookings and occupancy rates, particularly during peak season and school holidays when many families with pets are looking for a holiday destination. This can translate into increased revenue and profits for the property owner. Additionally, having a dog-friendly property can help to differentiate it from other properties in the market, making it more attractive to a wider range of guests. It can also help to build a loyal customer base who appreciate the ability to bring their pets with them on holiday.

Guests with pets may be more likely to stay for longer periods, which can help to increase occupancy rates and reduce the time that the property is left empty.

We can and do put restrictions and costs in place to safeguard the property. These include:

  • pet fee charge, to cover any additional cleaning or deodorising costs
  • size restrictions. Most of our dog friendly properties have a 10kg or less limit for the dog staying. If a property was large and had a secure spacious yard and this suited the property owners we can remove the size restriction
  • dogs are never allowed on furniture or beds. This is detailed in all messaging to guests and followed up by our cleaners
  • dogs are never to be left in the house alone, under any circumstances

Offering a dog-friendly short-stay holiday property can be beneficial for both guests and property owners. Guests can enjoy the benefits of bringing their pets along on holiday, while property owners can increase bookings, revenue, and loyalty. It can increase occupancy rates, and differentiate it from other properties in the market. However, it is important to ensure that the property is appropriately equipped and maintained to accommodate pets, and that guests are made aware of any rules or restrictions associated with bringing their pets along with them.
